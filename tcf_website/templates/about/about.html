theCourseForum

theCourseForum is a place for University of Virginia students to share their academic experiences from classes taken at UVA. The objective being to spread wisdom and knowledge between generations of Wahoos who find themselves struggling to pick classes during enrollment. We see this website as an opportunity to gain a student perspective on specific courses and their professors.

Of the students, by the students, for the students...



theCourseForum

connecting you to the courses you love.

theCourseForum is a product of developers and contributors from the UVA community. It's a place where students can share their academic experiences of classes they've taken. We provide past grade distributions along with student reviews for courses in all departments. Gain insight on which professors to choose and discover whether a class is worth a spot in your ideal schedule. We'll connect you to courses you'll love, and make enrollment stress free.

What can you do?

The biggest thing you can do: Submit reviews! While the website is spectacular, what really makes theCourseForum is the sharing of individual, personal experiences from UVA students. Write a review today to leave an impact on your community.
