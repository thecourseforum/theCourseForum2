theCourseForum

theCourseForum is a place for University of Virginia students to share their academic experiences from classes taken at UVA. The objective being to spread wisdom and knowledge between generations of Wahoos who find themselves struggling to pick classes during enrollment. We see this website as an opportunity to gain a student perspective on specific courses and their professors.
